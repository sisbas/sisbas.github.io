Smart oxyhealth

Since medical diagnostic devices and biochemical approaches provide information about the macro physiological environment, -but not events at the cellular level-, the cellular processes can only be predicted rather than proven. Smartphone-based cellular information approaches can provide rapid information at the cellular level, which is used in many places, such as to save time for early and accurate intervention and to evaluate body performance.​​ SMART OXY-HEALTH is an algorithm-based advanced software technology that is not affected by camera type, ambient light, distance, and angle between camera and object. SMART OXY-HEALTH can provide information about your cellular and microvascular health by using any smartphone. Clinicians, clinical research professionals, sportsmen-coaches, and general home users can use SMART OXY-HEALTH to understand the oxygen metabolism from basic to deep vision.​​
